Quick chicken mince stir-fry

Preparation time:

Total time required: 10mins

Ingredients needed:

  • 500g Coles RSPCA Approved Australian Chicken Mince
  • 1 pkt tri-colour capsicums, seeded, thinly sliced/li>
  • 450g pkt hokkien noodles
  • 120g honey soy chicken stir-fry sauce
  • 1 bunch choy sum, coarsely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat a wok over high heat. Add the mince and stir-fry with a flat-edged wooden spoon for 5 mins or until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Add capsicum to the wok. Stir-fry for 2 mins or until tender. Return mince to the wok with noodles and stir-fry sauce. Stir-fry for 5 mins or until heated through. Add choy sum and toss to combine. Divide among serving bowls.
